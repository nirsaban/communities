Batch C — Staff Roles

The people who run the room.

Two scoped staff roles. The Event Manager owns the day-of operations for events they're assigned to — attendees, materials, broadcasts, Q&A, recap. The Sub Admin runs the community day-to-day with a deliberate boundary: full member & content tooling, but no financial access — every money surface is replaced by a "Limited admin" guard.
